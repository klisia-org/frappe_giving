### Frappe Giving

Donation management for Frappe/ERPNext. Requires Payments from [Frappe](https://github.com/frappe/payments) or from our own [fork](https://github.com/klisia-org/payments) - which adds MoMo MTM.

### Features
The WHO:
Donor base management with links to Customer (ERPNext) and Contact (CRM)
Donor may have customer id in multiple Payment Gateways.
Donors receive user information after donation, with links to the Donation Portal, where they can get receipts, yearly statements, manage donation subscriptions - and , yes, give.

The WHAT:
Donation Campaigns: You can have Portal campaigns or Desk (in-person), with a distinct PURPOSE, Payment Gateway, Account (Chart of Accounts), Cost Center, and ERP Item.

The HOW:
Campaign Forms: One Donation Campaign may have multiple Campaign Forms, so you can do A/B testing or even just to use different visuals. Forms have their own title, image, subtitle, CTA label, description, and Thank you message (after a successful donation). You can also configure giving levels, toggle fields, show currency recovery text with the $ or %


### Installation

You can install this app using the [bench](https://github.com/frappe/bench) CLI:

```bash
cd $PATH_TO_YOUR_BENCH
bench get-app $URL_OF_THIS_REPO 
bench --site $YOUR_SITE install-app frappe_giving
```
